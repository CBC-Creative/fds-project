Cleaning for Health: What You’re Probably Missing

In this webinar, air quality expert Jeff Dryfhout explains growing occupant expectations of cleanliness in facilities, as well as how healthier and cleaner buildings provide a significant return on investment.

Learning objectives:

  • The role of air in infection control and overall employee health and wellness.
  • How to remove germs, allergens, and odors to create a cleaner, healthier environment.
  • The short- and long-term returns on investment of providing cleaner and healthier facilities for building occupants.
