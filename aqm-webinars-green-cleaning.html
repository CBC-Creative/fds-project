About the presenter: Mike Booth is Senior Global Marketing Manager for the Air Treatment category at Fellowes Brands. He has spent the last three years evangelizing the need to focus on indoor air quality and the impact it can have on people’s health and well-being. He is a seven-year veteran at Fellowes, and has worked in various marketing capacities with a focus on productivity and health in commercial environments.

Green Cleaning

Take your facility to the next level of clean with our on-demand webinar

In this informative session, you’ll learn why green cleaning is important to build and maintain indoor air quality and healthy, productive environments. You’ll come away with newfound knowledge and a blueprint for future success, as well as an easy-to-implement plan to take your facility into the next generation of Cleaning for Health.

You’ll Learn:

  • Learn how green cleaning can improve the health of occupants in any facility.
  • Discuss how a holistic approach to cleaning can save time and money.
  • Explore a step-by-step plan to implement a green cleaning program.
  • Identify ways to build on green cleaning successes and get next-generation clean.
