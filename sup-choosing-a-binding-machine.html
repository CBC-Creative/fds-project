Choosing a Binding Machine

What Binding Style Do You Need?

Comb Binding: Traditional finish, perfect for reports and presentations

  • Handles documents up to 500 pages
  • Papers can be easily added or removed
  • Lies flat when open for easy presenting or copying

Wire Binding: Stylish, metal finish

  • Permanent, secure binding
  • Lies flat when open
  • Bound pages rotate 360 degrees

Thermal Binding: Strong. Light compact

  • Squared back for easy on-shelf storage
  • Quick and easy to use
  • Binds multiple documents at once
  • Lies flat for mailing

How Often Will You Use Your Binder?

Occasional Use- For home or small office use. Bind documents no larger than 150 pages a few times a month.  See Our Recommended Binding Machine.

Moderate Use- For small office users that need to bind documents no larger than 300 pages a few times a week.  See Our Recommended Binding Machine.

Frequent Use- For office users that need to bind documents on a daily basis.  See Our Recommended Binding Machine.
