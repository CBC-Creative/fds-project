WHAT DRIVES US & INSPIRES US

Every day brings new opportunities to bring about a positive impact for our team members, partners and customers.

Here are the qualities that drive everything we do.

Family Innovation Quality Care

Serving at the intersection of Family, Innovation, Quality, & Care.

Since 1917, Fellowes has operated from the revolutionary belief that impact should be measured beyond our balance sheet, and that living out our convictions would be the most trustworthy guide toward lasting success.

Even as we have grown in scale, our entire team remains as unified and committed as ever to embody the same values established over a century ago: serving customers and the wider world with creativity, passion, and genuine partnership.

Family

Bound by common ties.

In more ways than one, we are a family. And we choose to operate as only a family can – through a culture of trust, connection, commonality, and joy.

  • Ambitious work.

    Big dreams have a home at Fellowes. We empower one-another to chase our potential with wide-eyed ambition, and the full support of our leadership teams.

  • Authentic connection.

    We believe that only when you bring all of yourself to your work, can you do the work no one else can do.

  • Uncommon challenges.

    Unleashing the worklife potential of everyone is no small task. When we band together as a global team working towards this common goal, there’s no limit to what we can accomplish.

Inno vation

Imagining the “not yet”, then creating it.

Since our inception, we have believed that the only way to deliver revolutionary solutions is to challenge ourselves far before our competition challenges us. This journey has led us to constantly reevaluate preconceived notions and the status quo. We believe that the best ideas are always yet to come, and always strive for imagining what may lie just beyond our current solutions, services, and capabilities.

FELLOWES INNOVATION AT WORK

quality

Building things that last, and not just products.

Quality isn’t simply how well a capability is manufactured or implemented. It’s an essence, a sense of depth, that assures what a company is offering the world was designed and created with thoughtfulness and skill. By strategically investing in quality at every corner of our organization, we are laying a firm foundation of quality that continues to pay back lasting dividends.

  • 500,000sqft of company-owned, company-operated manufacturing facilities.
  • An average 100-day testing period before a product is released.
  • Corporate training to up-skill every employee to new roles and responsibilities.
  • 100+ product design awards, averaging 3 per year.
  • Vertically-integrated supply chain to maintain consistent quality.

Care

Putting belief into practice.

For our legacy is to matter at all, it must first matter to us. We strive to approach each day with a posture of care for our work, our community, and our world.

We believe in people.

We see people are more than resources or assets, but as invaluable human beings capable of incredible things. We seek to empower every person, whether inside our organization or outside of it, to unleash their worklife potential.

We believe in ourselves.

Fellowes has stood the test of time, in part, because there is still so much left to do. We believe that we are the only organization on the planet who can do what we do, and we will never be satisfied until we prove it.

We believe impact multiplies.

When we live out our values and convictions, it gives others the courage to do the same.

Giving Back to the Places and Communities We Call Home

At Fellowes, we think carefully about our responsibility to build a healthier, more equitable world for everyone.
