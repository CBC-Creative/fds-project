Where Can I Find My CRC Number?

You an find this number on the bottom of your product or on the package that your product came in.

It will be indentified with the letters “CRC” and then a number with 5 digits or more. If you are registering a shredder you can find the CRC on the underside of the cutter or shredder head.

If you are having trouble locating your CRC, please contact Customer Service at 1-800-955-0959, Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM CT.
