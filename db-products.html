Products & Design

Fellowes has a rich history marked by creating the products, systems, and spaces where people can do their best work.

Work

We’re shaping the world of work. And the world of work shapes us.

Fellowes has always been focused on people, and the work they do. More specifically, how they do it, and where they do it. Even from our first product innovations, we’ve brought new efficiency, comfort, and safety to the spaces where work happens.

As early pioneers of human-centered product design, our journey led us to an expanded vision of our role in creating healthier workplaces, and a deeper understanding of how indoor environments should be catered to the needs of the people that use them, not the other way around.

Integrated In-House Product Development

Every product undergoes a highly regimented and robust process to ensure it meets Fellowes standards. Learn more about how we put products into the world.

    Process

    The first stage of our product design process is marked by conceptual ideation. We ask: “How can we think differently about new innovations, processes, systems, or environments? Where is there room for improvement or simplification? How can a category be redefined? Even better, how can we create a new one altogether?”

    It is only through rigorous exploration that we can arrive at new solutions that move worklife forward.
