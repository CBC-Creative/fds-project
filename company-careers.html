Working at Fellowes

Working at Fellowes means being part of a supportive and compassionate team - a family - and feeling inspired to bring innovation to the workplace every day. The idea of caring deeply about helping people be at their best goes back to our very beginning and still drives our mission today: creating spaces that foster productivity, organization, peace of mind and overall wellness. Ultimately, we help change consumers’ lives for the better. And that feels good.

Why Us? It’s Simple

In a world of increasing work/life integration, we’d rather focus on WorkLife balance. That means providing an environment and workplace focused on your needs as much as the business’s. That plays out in many ways, both large and small:

  • Competitive salary and benefits - comprehensive insurance offerings, 401(k) matching, company and individual performance incentives
  • Time off for giving back - building strong communities, improving lives and providing a better tomorrow is a hallmark of our culture
  • Summer hours to support your well-being and balance
  • A commitment to sustainability and driving green initiatives
  • A wellness program to help you achieve a more healthful lifestyle
  • Educational subsidies for further professional development
  • Casual dress
