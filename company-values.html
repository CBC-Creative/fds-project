Serving at the Intersection of
Family, Innovation, Quality, & Care.

Since 1917, we have operated from the belief that we are here to help improve the lives of those we serve. Still owned and led by the Fellowes family, our culture remains unified around values that were established over a century ago by our founder Harry Fellowes. Today, Fellowes is serving professionals across the world with innovative solutions that enable greater productivity, wellness and inspiration – Unleashing WorkLife Potential.

FAMILY
BOUND BY COMMON TIES

Informed by our roots as a generational family-owned business, family and relationships are priorities at Fellowes. Together, we are bonded through common values and standards of excellence. At the same time, we are stronger as a result of unique perspectives and skillsets. Teamwork at Fellowes embraces the collective and the individual at the same time.

INNOVATION
Imagining The “Not Yet”, Then Creating It.

Since our inception, we have believed that the only way to deliver revolutionary solutions is to challenge ourselves before and beyond our competition. This approach has allowed us to surpass preconceived notions and transcend the status quo as we assess and improve our solutions, services, and processes. We believe that the best ideas are yet to come through creativity and imagination. We are on the never-ending journey to make things distinctively better.

QUALITY
Doing Things Right

Inspired by our founder, excellence is our pursuit at each turn. When we are at our best, our customers and clients benefit. Whether it be our services, products, or relationships, we concentrate on each touchpoint of our brand experience to ensure quality is felt. When we fall short, we are diligent about pausing, identifying the core issue, and implementing systematic improvements – making us better.

CARE
A Company With A Conscience

We believe in going beyond the what and embracing the how. Whether it be our people and culture, interaction with our communities, or our impact on the planet, we take our responsibilities and what we contribute to the world seriously. We believe in respect and equality of opportunity for our people, we care about fostering a place of work that is magnetic and positive. We are grateful when we experience success, which enables us to use our platform to make the world a little better through giving back.
