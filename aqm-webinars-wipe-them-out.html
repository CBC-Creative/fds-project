Wipe ‘Em Out: How to Address Odors, Germs, and Complaints from Your Facility’s Restroom

Restroom Cleaning: A Plan to Better Health.

Odors and germs are a significant source of frustration for any facility professional and can negatively impact occupants and visitors' perceptions. Do away with complaints by following these easy-to-implement steps outlined in this eye-opening webinar tackling odors and germs in restrooms.

You’ll Learn:

  • Little known facts about germ spread in restrooms that can change the way you approach cleaning.
  • The impact of technology to reduce odor complaints and make housekeeping easier.
  • Issues around restroom air fresheners and the use volatile organic compounds.
  • Techniques to eliminate odors, not just mask them.
