    Breakthrough Innovations

    Optional Trim Designs

    Titan offers three trim profiles in a variety of colors and finishes that are easily interchangeable for low-cost, future updates. For a minimalistic design, select the low profile trim.

    Common Components Used in Many Places

    Titan is constructed around a Universal Track System™ that utilizes the same parts for a more streamlined, faster and easier installation.

    Convertible Single to Double Glazed Panes

    All of our trim options are designed to add glass panels without changing the underlying wall structure or track. In addition, the Titan dual glazing provides a 45 STC for greater acoustic privacy.

    Easy Integration

    Titan is designed to integrate with Fellowes furniture, case goods and air quality management solutions.

    Smarter for the Planet, Smarter for Your Business

    Titan walls are constructed with up to 90% less waste than traditionally-framed walls, cost less than traditional construction and are backed by an industry-leading lifetime warranty.

    Let's Get to Work

    In 1917, Harry Fellowes set in motion a way of doing business that has become our cultural foundation at Fellowes to this very day. We are here to serve, through a humble and helpful spirit. We strive each day to pursue our values of family, innovation, quality and care. Into our fourth generation, our work is driven by a purpose to add value to those we serve.

    Learn About Our Initiatives

    Established in 2014, GIVE is a purpose-led program dedicated to supporting local and global partners who are addressing humanitarian's greatest social, education and health-based needs..

    Learn About Our Initiatives

Grounded in a conviction that we have great responsibility in caretaking our generation's imprint on this world, the Earth framework is integrated into our product development process, packaging design, organizational facilities and policies as well as our daily decision-making.
