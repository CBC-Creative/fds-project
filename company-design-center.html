WHERE IT BEGAN
Just across the Chicago river from where Harry Fellowes opened the first office over 100 years ago, we’re excited to announce Fellowes brand expansion into Contract Interiors - back in the city where it all began.
THE VISION
We sought out to create a place to learn, test, touch, see and experience our products in a new way. A place to create environments that unleash worklife potential through solutions that deliver on wellness, productivity and inspiration.
FULTON MARKET
This thriving neighborhood is filled with growing businesses, restaurants and an expanding Contract Interiors industry. Fellowes is proud to foster our continued growth by creating a brand experience in the heart of Fulton Market.
