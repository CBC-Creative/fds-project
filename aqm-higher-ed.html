    High Performance, Low Profile

    • Array Recessed units' energy efficient design includes our unique mixed-flow fan, designed to maximize air flow while delivering quiet operation
    • Available in 2 sizes, single unit covers 750 square feet and double unit covers 1,500 square feet

    Wall & Stand
    Wall to Wall Protection
    Array™ Wall can be installed on a range of wall materials with minimal installation requirements.
    • Clean, subtle design includes integrated Lookout display providing Air Quality status to users of an area
    • Available in 2 sizes, AW1 unit covers 600 square feet and AW2 unit covers 1000 square feet
    • Designed for Maximum Flexibility - Wall units are easily installed to walls and the stand units can be rapidly moved and repositioned wherever needed, no installation required
    Ideal for Spaces Like:
    • Study Areas
    • Staff Offices
    • Common Areas
    Recess UVC
    Light powered disinfection in a stylish flush mount design that fits perfectly into a standard 2x4 grid ceiling
    • Specially designed to provide continuous 24x7 ultraviolet protection against airborne bacteria, viruses and mold
    • Self-contained UV-C chamber remains hidden in the ceiling plenum
    • Up to 98.1% Single Pass Kill Rate*
    • 9,000 hour bulb lasts 1-year

    *Based on 98.1% Single Pass Reduction in Staphylococcus epidermidis bacteria. The term “Kill” is used to represent the inactivation of pathogens from the UV-C light.

    Ideal for Spaces Like:
    • Restrooms
    • Science Labs
    • Health Offices
    • Locker Rooms
