    Discover The Science Behind The Array System And Learn How It Works

    Seamless & Scalable Integration

    Array air purification system is designed to fit seamlessly into any space and can provide localized air quality management throughout your building.

    It is also highly adaptable and can be scaled to meet the needs of your building’s structure, number of occupants, and purification requirements.

    Automatic Sense & React Technology

    Array comes with a suite of Envirosmart+™ sensors that are designed to monitor and respond to changes in the indoor quality in your building. These sensors allow our units to work hard when they need to, and then shift to standby mode, when possible, in order to conserve energy and reduce operating expenses.

    • Every purifier and sensor continuously monitors and visualizes trends of the top 9 air quality factors so that you can easily monitor changes in Indoor Air Quality and correlate them to your building performance.
    • Our full suite of IAQ, monitoring and comfort sensors include particulate (PM2.5, PM10), VOC/TVOCs, CO2, RH, temperature, indoor air pressure, light, occupancy and sound.
    • Every unit independently and securely connects to the cloud utilizing LTE which means that you won’t need to have a local network in order to use our technology.

    Effective & Efficient Filtration

    Our 3-in-1 filter is 99.95% effective in capturing particles as small as 0.1 microns including viruses, allergens and germs. The proprietary mixed-flow fan design maximizes air flow while delivering quiet operation.

    Monitoring & Real-Time Data with Viewpoint

    Array provides you with smart air purification. With intuitive monitoring and display technologies, you can view the exact air quality status of your space in real-time through the Viewpoint Display

    • Viewpoint displays concurrent data, notifications, trends and information that enables optimal indoor Air Quality Management. Available with any Array device.
    • Building managers can schedule maintenance needs, track air quality over time, and identify correlations with energy usage*.
    • Array’s Lookout display shows precise air quality status and filtration progress via a wall-mounted or desktop design.

    *Available to Viewpoint Plus subscribers
